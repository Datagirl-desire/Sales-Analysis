### Project Overview

This Data Analysis project aims to provide insights on the sales performance of a coffee shop chain using their transactional data for a period of time. by analyzing various aspect of the transactional data, we seek to extract insights on product popularity, revenue generation, customer purchasing patterns, and operational efficiency across various stores and time periods. This analysis will provide valuable business intelligence for the coffee shop chain.

### Data Source

Sales Data: The primary dataset used for this analysis is "coffee shop csv" file, containing detailed infromation about each transactional sales made by the company

### Exploratory Data Analysis

EDA involved exploring the transactional data to answer key questions, such as:

- Top 5 most frequently sold products by product category
- Monthly revenue generated
- product variety in specific locations
- Transaction timing analysis
- Average revenue per transaction for each product category during Peak and Non-Peak hours
- what is the product with the most price flunctuations
- what is the product availability across all stores
- Transactional quantity deviation
- what is the product sales efficiency

### Data Analysis

Include some interesting code/features worked with

```sql
SELECT
    product_category, product_id,
    sum(transaction_qty) AS total_qty_sold
FROM transactions
GROUP BY product_category, product_id
ORDER BY total_qty_sold DESC
LIMIT 5;
```
